About Our Cause

Climate change is the long-term alteration of temperature and typical weather patterns in a place. Climate change could refer to a particular location or the planet as a whole. Climate change has been happening for billions of years. Humans are playing a big role in climate change, primarily by adding CO2 and other greenhouse gases to the atmosphere.
